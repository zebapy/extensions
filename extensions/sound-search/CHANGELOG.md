# Sound Search Changelog

## [Initial Version] - 2025-11-07

### Added
- Core command: **Search for Sounds** – Quickly find sound or music samples by genre from available sources.
- AI Command: **Ask Sound Search** – Use natural language or prompts to search for music and sound samples; the extension automatically maps your query to the best genres.
- New Sound Source: **Soundraw** – Integration with Soundraw's 100% Copyright-Safe AI Music Generator (currently available in closed beta).
- **Favorites** – Mark your favorite samples and access them anytime in the Favorites section.
- **Recently Added** – Quickly access your most recently discovered samples from the Recents section.
